About Us

Malawian Scholars Of Turkey association is an independent, peaceful, democratic, non-political, non-profit association committed to the interests of Malawian students and graduates from Turkish academic institutions (based in Turkey) and the ultimate benefit of the society. MAST is an association of Malawian students currently studying in turkey and Malawian graduates from Turkish universities.

UNITED

Creating a bridge between Malawian students in Turkey and strengthen the relationships with our country.

COOPERATIVE

Establish a social environment with "Collaboration", "Fairness" & "Inclusivity".

LINKED

MAST provides a space for its members to showcase their skills & experiences in order to attract job offers & networking groups.

INTEGRATION

We enable integration through effective communication, streamlines operations and promoting a holistic approach to problem-solving and decision-making.
